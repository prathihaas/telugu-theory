Profile system

How the grid composes itself.

The feed is laid out as a checkerboard — color block / photo / type — repeating every three posts. Highlights match the secondary palette so they read as a single ribbon. Bio is exactly three lines, in this order: tagline, location, hours.

  • Avatar · always the yellow-outline mark on red, never the wordmark.
  • Highlights · six only — Biryani, Tiffin, Andhra, Kitchen, Reviews, Stories.
  • Tone · house rules, single-line quotes, numbers + facts.
  • No emoji in captions. Telugu lines welcome.

Three rules of the grid

Treat the feed as one designed object.

01

Never two reds in a row.

Break colour blocks with photo or cream.

02

One reel per row, max.

Reels show as black tiles; one keeps rhythm.

03

End every row with type.

Words, numbers or a quote — never a photo.
